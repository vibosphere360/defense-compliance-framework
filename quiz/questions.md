# ITAR/EAR/CUI Compliance Quiz (15 Questions)
## Instructions: 12/15 to pass, open notes allowed

1. Which agency controls military-grade drones? → DDTC (State Department)
2. What is a "deemed export"? → Showing ITAR data to foreign national inside U.S.
3. Which does NOT verify U.S. person status? → H-1B Work Visa
4. How long retain ITAR records? → 5 years
5. German intern + CAD drawing? → License required before sharing
6. Red flag end user? → Military in embargoed country
7. Maximum penalty for ITAR violation? → $1M + 20 years
8. Safe location for ITAR data? → Company OneDrive (ITAR-configured, U.S.-hosted)
9. Found a violation? → Stop, document, report immediately
10. 5-Second Classification Test asks? → "Was this designed primarily for military use?"
11. 10% U.S. content to Iran? → Subject to EAR (de minimis does NOT apply)
12. Publication delays on research? → May void fundamental research exclusion
13. Retiring ITAR hard drive? → Destroy (shred/incinerate)
14. Customer on Entity List? → Obtain license or deny sale
15. Discovered violation from 6 months ago? → File Voluntary Self-Disclosure immediately
